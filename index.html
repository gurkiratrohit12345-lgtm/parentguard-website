Verification & trust

Every caretaker verified
before they meet your parent.

We don't rely on word-of-mouth. Every caretaker passes a rigorous 5-layer verification — regardless of which city they're in.

1

Aadhaar eKYC

Identity verified against UIDAI in real time. Tokenized storage only — raw Aadhaar number never stored. DPDP-compliant.

2

Police verification certificate

Mandatory for Tier 2 and 3 caretakers across all cities. State-specific police verification supported.

3

State nursing council check

License verified against respective State Nurses Registration Council database for Tier 2 and 3.

4

Reference calls to employers

Our ops team calls two previous employers using a structured 10-question script. Not just names on a form.

5

Tier assessment test

20-question clinically validated test per tier. Caretakers only certified for tiers they pass. No exceptions.

🛡️

GPS-verified every shift

Every shift starts with GPS check-in at your parent's address. No show = instant alert to you and no payment to caretaker.

📊

Automated vital alerts

BP, blood sugar, pulse, SpO2 logged at set intervals. Out-of-range readings trigger automatic guardian alerts.

⭐

Rating-gated payments

Your shift rating is required before payment releases. Direct accountability on every single shift.

🔒

Data stored only in India

All health data on AWS Mumbai. Full encryption. Data deletion honoured within 30 days. Full DPDP Act 2023 compliance.

Everything you need to know

Every caretaker passes Aadhaar eKYC, police background check, reference calls, and a clinical tier assessment before their first shift. You also get real-time GPS check-in, vital sign logging, task tracking, and a full SOS emergency protocol. No local agency or WhatsApp referral gives you any of this — anywhere in India.
All health data is stored exclusively in India (AWS Mumbai), encrypted at rest and in transit. Only you and your assigned caretaker can see your parent's profile. ParentGuard never sells or shares health data. You can request complete data deletion at any time under the DPDP Act 2023, processed within 30 days.
If a caretaker doesn't GPS clock-in at the scheduled time, you receive an automatic alert immediately. Our ops team begins sourcing a replacement. Caretakers who miss shifts face suspension. You are never billed for a shift that didn't happen — guaranteed.
You register as a guardian remotely — takes under 15 minutes. Pay in GBP, CAD, AUD, or USD. Add a local family member as an on-ground contact. Once care starts, you see everything in real time in your timezone. Our NRI Premium plan includes international payment, multi-timezone notifications, and multi-family access.
Download the app, select "I'm a caretaker" and register. Submit your documents, complete Aadhaar verification, and our ops team reviews within 48 hours. Once police verification is done and your badge activates, you can accept shifts in your city. Full process takes 7–14 days.
We're currently live in Ludhiana and actively expanding. Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, and Ahmedabad are in our near-term plan. Join the city waitlist above to get early access when we launch near you.
